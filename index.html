About Me

My name is Enes Kemal and I am currently working towards my PhD in University of British Columbia. My research interest are all the way from cognitive artificial intelligence to medical applications of AI, from Bioinformatics to Astrophysics. So, what I say about myself is that I love exploring new horizons in different fields. Currently I am fascinated by Proteomics and it's applications. I am working as a graduate assistant in Lange Lab and hoping to explore more about Proteomics field.
